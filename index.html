About Us

Chris Powers, President

Licenced General Contractor
Serving the Treasure Coast

As a South Florida Resident of 40 years, Chris Powers, President of Powerhouse Property Services, has been part of the construction industry in Florida for most of his life. He spent his teens learning the remodeling business from his family in high end residential neighborhoods in the intracoastal and beach areas of Broward and Palm Beach Counties. He always knew his life would revolve around real estate and dreamed of opening up his own business. Summers in Florida consisted of hard work learning the carpentry and finish trades in the Florida heat; installing doors in commercial buildings and performing form carpentry for residential home builders.

Once Chris graduated from Florida Atlantic University with a Bachelors of Science degree in Political Science, he continued to pursue his career path as a superintendant for GL Homes, one of South Florida's largest homebuilders. He then worked with Levitt and Sons managing their customer service department. Finally, Chris moved to the boardroom as DR Horton's Director of Warranty and Quality Control. Each step taught him more and more the value of excellent customer service. This is the relentless drive for excellence which embodies the Powerhouse building experience.
